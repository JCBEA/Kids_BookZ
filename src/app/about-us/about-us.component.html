Mission

As part of our mission students who come from communities with a lack of resources will be encouraged to develop a love of reading, improved literacy skills, and support for their social and emotional well-being.

Vision

The vision of Read to a Child is to provide every child with the opportunity to succeed in school and in life by providing them with inspirational mentoring and by providing them with read-aloud experiences.

Values

Read to a Child was founded on the belief that every child deserves to have a caring adult read to them on a regular basis. Our strategy is based on the assumption that a lifelong love of reading is a driver of opportunity for children throughout their lives. A caring relationship with a reading mentor promotes the attention, engagement, and confidence that all children require to thrive.
